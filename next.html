About Us

NayePankh Foundation is a youth-driven NGO committed to transforming lives through education, food distribution, women empowerment, and social awareness programs.

Our mission is to ensure that every child gets education, every family gets food support, and every individual gets equal opportunity to grow.

We work across multiple cities with volunteers who dedicate their time for social good.
